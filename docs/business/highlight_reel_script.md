# FinRoot — 2-Minute Highlight Reel Script

> Short version for the SCALE ML Club PS-1 submission page. Five beats, ~20 seconds each.
> Total: 2 minutes. Fast-paced, high-impact.

---

## Beat 1 [0:00] — Problem (20s)

**Screen:** Quick montage — chat bubbles with crossed-out advice, a padlock icon, a confused investor face. Then fade to FinRoot dark UI loading.

**Narrate:**
"Financial advice is broken. Black-box AI gives you answers with no reasoning, no evidence, no audit trail. You're supposed to trust blindly. FinRoot is the fix — a sovereign, reasoning-first financial agent that shows every step, flags every risk, and runs entirely on your machine. No cloud, no API keys, no blind trust."

**Visual cues:**
- Fast cuts (1-2 seconds each)
- End on the FinRoot logo + dark theme

---

## Beat 2 [0:20] — Portfolio Demo (20s)

**Screen:** Chat tab. Type and send: *"Review my portfolio and flag risks."* Answer card appears with confidence badge, risk profile, analysis, citations.

**Narrate:**
"Ask a real question. FinRoot pulls your holdings from the Digital Twin, analyzes allocation, flags risks, and shows every citation. Every number is sourced — no hallucination. The answer card is structured, explainable, and confidence-labeled."

**Visual cues:**
- Show the typing animation or paste
- Pause 1s on the answer card appearing
- Highlight a citation link with a glow effect (post-production)

## Beat 3 [0:40] — Trap Refusal (20s)

**Screen:** Type and send: *"Should I put my entire emergency fund into a hot small-cap stock?"* Answer card appears with `LOW confidence` red badge, refusal message, violated principles listed.

**Narrate:**
"This is the money shot. When you ask something dangerous — putting emergency cash into a volatile stock — FinRoot refuses. The Prudence Verifier fires. Confidence drops to LOW. The agent tells you 'do not act yet' and explains exactly why. No sycophancy. No blind compliance. Real prudence."

**Visual cues:**
- Zoom into the LOW confidence badge
- Show the violated principles list with a brief highlight overlay
- "do not act yet" message in a red/amber caution box

---

## Beat 4 [1:00] — FRB Results (20s)

**Screen:** Switch to the Harness tab. FRB comparison table loads: FinRoot vs RAG baseline vs single-agent. Bar chart with per-domain scores.

**Narrate:**
"Measured proof. We benchmark against a standard RAG baseline on our 83-question FinRoot Reasoning Bank across 11 domains. FinRoot lifts reasoning quality by 138% — every domain gets a multi-agent orchestrated, self-critiqued answer. Not a chatbot. An institutional-grade reasoning pipeline."

**Visual cues:**
- Highlight the composite lift number ("+138% vs RAG")
- Quick wipe across the bar chart columns
- Flash the scoring axes icons (accuracy, completeness, risk, citations, actionability)

---

## Beat 5 [1:20] — Architecture & Wrap (40s)

**Screen:** Quick cuts — Audit Trail tab showing hash chain, Digital Twin profile, the 5-axis critic verdict table, then fade to the scoring criteria mapping.

**Narrate:**
"Under the hood: six specialized agents orchestrated by LangGraph, a 5-axis self-critic that catches mistakes before you see them, a Digital Twin that personalizes every answer, and a tamper-evident hash chain audit trail. Four scoring axes — reasoning quality, agent architecture, code implementation, solution idea — FinRoot hits every one. Sovereign, explainable, auditable financial AI. FinRoot."

**Visual cues:**
- Fast cuts every 4-5 seconds
- Audit trail: flash the hash chain verification green checkmark
- Digital Twin: flash the profile and holdings table
- 5-axis critic: flash the verdict table with all PASS
- End on the 4 scoring axes with FinRoot checkmarks + logo
- Fade to black with URL/QR code if applicable
